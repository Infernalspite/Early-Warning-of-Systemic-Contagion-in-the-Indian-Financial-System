About the Signals

CRI Score (0–100) operates on three predicted regimes:

  • Normal Regime (< 30): Average volatility, normal interbank correlations, standard repo rate adjustments.
  • Pre-Crisis Regime (30–60): 30-day window prior to major default/regulatory action. Often characterized by rupee depreciation and rising interbank correlation.
  • Crisis Regime (>= 60): Market crash during which Nifty Bank falls sharply and interbank contagion spreads.
